About RoundZer0

Welcome to RoundZer0, the platform revolutionizing the hiring process with AI-driven initial interviews. Our mission is to give everyone an equal opportunity to showcase their skills, regardless of prior commercial experience, and help employers identify the best talent efficiently.

How It Works

At RoundZer0, candidates apply for job openings and participate in the "Round Zero" interview, conducted entirely by AI. These interviews evaluate candidates based on predefined criteria tailored to the job role, ensuring an unbiased and fair assessment.

Why RoundZer0?

Many talented individuals miss out on opportunities due to lack of commercial experience or traditional hiring biases. RoundZer0 levels the playing field by allowing candidates to prove their abilities in the very first round, giving everyone a fair shot at advancing to the next stage of the hiring process.

Empowering Employers

For employers, RoundZer0 streamlines the hiring process by shortlisting top candidates based on AI interview evaluations. This allows recruiters to focus on meaningful face-to-face interactions in subsequent interview rounds, saving time and resources.

Our Vision

We envision a future where job opportunities are accessible to everyone, and hiring decisions are driven by talent and potential, not just resumes. RoundZer0 is committed to building a fair, inclusive, and efficient hiring ecosystem for both candidates and employers.
